Experiences

  • Jump Inc Trampoling Park
    Jump Inc is a indoor trampoling park! Jump Inc Trampoline Park Sheffield is a 24,000 sq ft spring-loaded urban playground. Visit their website for more information. Sheffield 101 top tip: visit on a week day evening for a reduced entry fee!
  • The Peak District
    The Peak DIstriction National Park is just a bus journey away from Sheffield Station. It is best place if you want to go on a scenic hike or take stunning photographs of the British countryside. Sheffield 101 top tip: whether summer or winter, views are just as breathtaking! Stop for a pub lunch, visit Chatsworth House or take your disposable BBQ for a relaxing day out. The Peak District National Park.
  • Meadwhall Shopping Centre
     Meadowhall is one of the largest shopping centre in the UK. To date, there are 280 stores and services. It hosts events all year around, but the one worth mentioning is the 'Student Night'. One night, twice a year, shops offer student discounts to whoever signed up for the night with Unidays. There are always deals and competitions that cannot be missed!. Sheffield 101 top tip: the shopping centre is just outside Sheffield. There are trains that stop there every 5 minutes and with your railcard, tickets are basically close to nothing.
