Nightlife


  • West Street Live
    West Street Live is a music venue and club situated in the busy West Street in Sheffield. It is open 7 days a week and it is where Uni of and Sheffield Hallam Students come together to dance and sing to some of their favourite songs. Visit their website for more information.
    Sheffield 101 top tip: gather some friends and head down to West Street on a Friday, Saturday or even a Sunday. You will not regret it and you will find yourself dancing until early hours of the morning!

  • Crystal Bar
    Crystal Bar is situated in Carver Street among some of Sheffield's most popular nightclubs. It is here though, where every single Wednesday, Sheffield Hallam University hosts is very own club night: Hallamnation. You can be sure you will hear Mr. Brightside every time, at the end of every night! Check out their Facebook page for events and tickets.
    Sheffield 101 top tip: look out for special events and themed nights. Make sure to buy your tickets in advance in order to avoid queuing for too long!

  • o2 Academy Sheffield
    Sheffield's o2 Academy is situated near the city centre. It hosts big and small acts, cover bands and special club nights. This is their Twitter page if you would like to find out more about past and upcoming events.
    Sheffield 101 top tip: follow the o2 on their social media to be the first one to know when your favourite band, singer or Dj is going to be in Sheffield!
