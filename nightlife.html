Nightlife

  • West Street Live
    West Street Live is a music venue and club situated in the busy West Street in Sheffield. It is open 7 days a week and it is where Uni of and Sheffield Hallam Students come together to dance and sing to some of their favourite songs. Visit their website for more information.
  • Crystal Bar
    Crystal Bar is situated in Carver Street among some of Sheffield's most popular nightclubs. It is here though, where every single Wednesday, Sheffield Hallam University hosts is very own club night: Hallamnation. You can be sure you will hear Mr. Brightside every time, at the end of every night! Check out their Facebook page for events and tickets.
  • o2 Academy Sheffield
    Sheffield's o2 Academy is situated near the city centre. It hosts big and small acts, cover bands and special club nights.
