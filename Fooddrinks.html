Food & Drinks

  • Peddlers market
    Peddlers' market, an indoor street food market is on the first Friday and Saturday of each month. Visit 92 Burton Road in Kelham Island on these dates for a evening of good food, drinks and live music. Visit their website for more information.
    Sheffield 101 top tip: Visit Sheffield brewery company across the road for a local beer on Peddler's night!
  • Marmadukes' cafe
    Marmadukes' is a lovely independent cafe with great coffee, brunch, lunch and snack options. Visit their website for more information. Sheffield 101 top tip: Try their homemade cheesecake, you won't regret it!
  • Bungalow and bears
    Visit Bungalow and bears, when you fancy a beer and some food in a relaxed environment, with frequent DJ sets playing in the background. Visit their website for more information. Sheffield 101 Top tip: Visit on a Tuesday evening for 2-1 burgers and sides!
